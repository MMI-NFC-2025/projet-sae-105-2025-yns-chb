About

Hello,

My name is Yanis Chouhib, and I am a first-year student in the BUT Métiers du Multimédia et de l'Internet (MMI) program in Montbéliard.

This website was created as part of a university project aimed at highlighting a woman scientist whose work was overlooked by a man, a phenomenon known as the "Matilda effect."

Through various articles, analyses, and cultural references (films, books, documentaries), this site seeks to celebrate the legacy of women in science and raise public awareness about the ongoing injustices in the recognition of their contributions to science.
